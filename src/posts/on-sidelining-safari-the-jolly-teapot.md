April 26, 2026

Yesterday, I read Nicolas Magand's [blog post](https://thejollyteapot.com/2026/04/24/sidelining-safari/), "Sidelining Safari," which I found interesting because I recently [switched to Firefox](https://ldstephens.net/posts/i-switched-to-firefox/) from Safari. He, too, switched, opting for [Helium](https://helium.computer), a Chromium-based browser. I decided to check it out and have since set it as my default browser. 

I have two issues with Firefox: its lack of AppleScript compatibility and its failure to integrate with Things' quick entry autofill. I use both of these features fairly often, and since they are compatible with Helium, it may be a better choice.
