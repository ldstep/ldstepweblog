[Apple Support:](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/force-restart-iphone-iph8903c3ee6/ios)

> If iPhone isn’t responding, and you can’t [turn it off then on](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/turn-iphone-on-or-off-iph841379c3d/26/ios/26#iph6c6b41f36), try forcing it to restart.
> 
> 1. Press and quickly release the volume up button.
> 2. Press and quickly release the volume down button.
> 3. Press and hold the side button.
> 4. When the Apple logo appears, release the side button.
