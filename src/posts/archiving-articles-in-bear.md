June 21, 2026

I saved a guide called *[npm: A Free Guide for Beginners](https://css-tricks.com/a-complete-beginners-guide-to-npm/)* to GoodLinks the other day. This morning, I wanted to read it and keep it for future reference, but I realized GoodLinks isn't where I want to store this kind of material.

I briefly thought about DEVONthink, but since I uninstalled it recently, it wasn't an option.

Instead, I saved it to Bear using the browser extension. It captures the entire article, allowing me to read, highlight, and archive everything in one place. 

It is another nice use for Bear.
