May 18, 2026

[Marked 3 is officially out](https://brettterpstra.com/2026/05/18/marked-3-is-officially-out/) 

> [Marked 2](https://marked2app.com/) will continue to be available for those who don’t need everything Marked 3 offers. It will always be a one-time purchase, but won’t see a lot of updates, other than to keep it running as new OS versions come out.

I’m a very light user of Marked and don’t really need the additional features in Marked 3, so I’m glad to see that Marked 2 will continue to be maintained for future macOS upgrades.
